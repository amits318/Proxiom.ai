Why Proxiom.ai?

Intake systems are universally deployed in Legal, HR, Sales, Customer Support, and other Enterprise Teams. Users want the flexibility to describe their issues in a natural way - voice or text. However, most NLP-based intake systems end up collecting vague, incomplete requests. This leads to delays, misrouting, missed upselling/crosselling opportunities and frustration.

At Proxiom.ai we are building expert agents that work like employees—learning, adapting, and mastering enterprise processes. Our mission is to redefine work by designing intelligent proxies that live the life cycle of a high-performing employee: they onboard, learn, have goals, adapt, and continuously grow. We are the intelligence behind every enterprise request.

Real-Time Feedback

Natural Language Input
