Promptly AI Studio

Promptly AI Studio — inspired by popular AI Studios like Meta's AI Studio, Google's Vertex AI Studio and Anthropic's Claude — but purpose-built for request intake. Promptly Studio enables operations, support, and revenue teams to design, test, and deploy their custom purpose specific intake validator agents that clarify, prioritize, and route requests across channels — without needing any coding or additional manpower. It combines LLM-powered understanding with structured business logic, giving teams full control over intake flows, channel configurations, escalation rules, observabilty tools and CRM integrations, all in one place.
